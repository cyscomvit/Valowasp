FAQs

>
What type of tournament is this?
For the Qualification Round, it is a knockout tournament. Later, depending on the teams for the semi-finals round, we will have fixtures based on your team's average combact score.

For Example:
Match 1
TEAM A VS TEAM B {(A WINNER) - Total Combact Socre - 2200}
Match 2
TEAM C VS TEAM D (C WINNER) - Total Combact Socre - 2300}
So TEAM C will be the table topper's followed by TEAM A.
What reward will be given to us?
  • A cash price of 1K will be awarded to the team by OWASP.
  • The team will get to feature in the hall of OWASPVIT
  • The winning team will be posted on Social Media Outposts.
I am an Iron Player. Will I get a Radiant player on the opponent's team?
No, you will not.Teams matched against other teams will be based on both account levels and the ranking of each player.
Each team will get a worthy opponent.
Will a tactical timeout be given to each team?
Yes, only 1 Tactical Timeout of 3 mins will be given to each team per game.
I'm having network issues. Can you reschedule my match?
No, the schedule will not be changed. However, if you are having network issues, make sure you have a backup network.
Can we draw the match?
Once the match has started, no one should draw the match if the match goes to OT (Over Time).
Will the match be streamed?
Yes, for the knockout tournament, the matches will be streamed on Discord only and the finals will be streamed on the OWASP Official Youtube Channel.
